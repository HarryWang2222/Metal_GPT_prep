Abstract: The effects of multiple microalloy additions on the dynamic recovery and recrystallization behavior of austenite were investigated in a series of 0.05% C-l.25% Mn steels. Abstract: The characteristics of dynamic recrystallization in <111 . single crystals of nickel were investigated and compared to those in copper.
