Abstract: Coarsening of the γ' precipitate in binary NiAl alloys containing 6.35 and 6.50 wt.% Al has been studied by measuring the ferromagnetic Curie temperature as a function of ageing time at 625 and 715°C. The concentration of Al is linearly related to t−13 at long ageing times, in agreement with theory. Analysis of the kinetics has provided values of ce, the concentration of Al in equilibrium with a γ' particle of infinite size. The values of ce are about 10% larger than the literature values of the solubility at the relevant temperatures. This suggests that the present values of ce, represent the coherent solubilities. The results of this investigation, combined with extant data describing the coarsening of γ' in terms of average particle size measurements, are used to exemplify a recently proposed method (A. J. Ardell, Acta Met.15, 1772 (1967) for obtaining reliable values of the particle-matrix interfacial free energy, γ, and the “effective” diffusivity of the solute in the matrix, Deff. As a result of the proposed method, the value of γ has been revised to approximately 14 erg/cm2 from a previous estimate of 30 erg/cm2. The value of Deff is greater than the (extrapolated) coefficient of diffusion of Al in very dilute Ni by a factor cf about two. Deff is also about a factor of two greater than that predicted by the application of irreversible thermodynamics to the process of volume-constrained growth and ungrowth of coherent precipitates during coarsening.

Abstract: The microstructural damage and its subsequent recovery in molybdenum, irradiated at 600°C, was studied by quantitative electron microscopy. The microstructure after irradiation consisted of large interstitial loops and smaller clusters within the grains. A zone denuded of interstitial loops, but containing vacancy clusters, was present near grain boundaries. The disappearance during annealing of the large interstitial loops within the grains occurred at a rate equal to that of self-diffusion, as did the growth of the vacancy loops within the denuded zones. The rapid annealing of small clusters within the grains occurred at a rate more nearly equal to that of vacancy migration.

Abstract: By making use of iron electrodes the thermoelectric power has been measured for ferrous oxide and ferrous oxide + silicate mixtures in the liquid and solid state and for molten ferrous oxide + calcium oxide mixtures. Abstract: Gold was evaporated in ultra-high vacuum (~5 × 10−9 torr) onto molybdenite substrates so as to produce thin films with a thickness gradient, ranging from very small discontinuous nuclei to a continuous layer. The angular misorientation of small gold nuclei, of radius ~20 Å, containing between 30 and 60 atoms, was determined by moiré fringe techniques, and was compared with the misorientation of larger nuclei (radius ~80 Å) as well as that of semi-continuous areas. Two substrate temperatures were employed, 100°C and 300°C, while the evaporation rate was kept constant at ~200 Å/min. Les bandes 3d des alliages Cu-Ni ne se remplissent pas complètement quand la concentration du cuivre devient voisine de 60% at. Le remplissage maximum a lieu à 57 % at. Cu. Les domaines d'énergies correspondant aux états inoccupés entre le niveau de Fermi et le sommet de la bande 3d varient à peu de choses près comme 3/2kT. Ces états inoccupés peuvent être interprétés à partir des électrons sd hybrides. Ainsi, les modèles à deux bandes pour expliquer le comportement des alliages Cu-Ni semblent être trop simples. L'addition d'autres éléments à l'alliage de base Cu-Ni paraît accentuer légèrement le chevauchement de la bande 4s par rapport à la bande 3d, et il semble que quelques niveaux 4s supplémentaires soient remplis aux dépens des états 3d. L'auteur propose des relations empiriques pour décrire les effets des additions d'éléments sur le domaine d'énergies situé entre le niveau de Fermi et le sommet de la bande 3d. Thermoelektrische Daten wurden herangezogen, um zu untersuchen, wie 4s-Elektronen der Cu-Atome die 3d-Bänder des Ni füllen. Die 3d-Bänder von Cu-Ni-Legierungen werden nicht vollständig gefüllt wenn sich der Cu-Gehalt 60 At.% nähert. Die 3d-Bänder sind bei 57 At. % Cu maximal gefüllt. Der Energiebereich der nicht besetzten Zustände zwischen der Fermienergie und der oberen Kante des 3d-Bands ändert sich näheruiigsweise wie 3/2 kT. Diese nicht besetzten Zustände können mit Hilfe von hybridisierten sd-Elektronen erklärt werden. Somit scheinen die Zweibändermodelle für das Verhalten von Cu-Ni-Legierungen zu einfach zu sein. Das Zufügen legierender Elemente zur Cu-Ni-Basis hat eine leichte Vertiefung des überlappenden Bereichs der 4s- und 3d-Bänder zur Folge und es scheint so, daβ eine kleine zusätzliche Zahl von 4s-Zuständen auf Kosten von 3d-Zuständen gefüllt ist. Eine empriische Beziehung zur Beschreibung des Einflusses der Zulegierung auf den Energiespalt zwischen der Fermienergie und der oberen Kante des 3d-Bandes wird angegeben.

Abstract: The effect on the Hugoniot elastic wave amplitude of varying the grain size by about an order of magnitude has been investigated with plane wave explosive loading experiments on SAE 1018 mild steel. The average grain diameter was varied in steps from 9.4 to 70.3 × 10−3 mm by annealing heat treatments. In agreement with commonly observed behavior, the upper and lower yield stresses obtained from quasistatic tensile tests were found to depend markedly on grain size; this served to standardize the material.
