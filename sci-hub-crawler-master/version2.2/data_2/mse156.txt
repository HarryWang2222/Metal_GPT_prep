Abstract: This study reports on the creep–fatigue interaction of the oxide dispersion strengthened Ni-base superalloy PM 1000. TMF tests resulted in unexpectedly low fatigue lives. Grain boundary cavitation observed in the in-phase TMF tests indicated that creep damage plays an important role under TMF loading conditions.
